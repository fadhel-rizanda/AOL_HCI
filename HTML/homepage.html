Frequently Asked Questions

What is Water Guard?
WaterGuard is an application that helps users monitor and report water quality in various locations. The app provides up-to-date information about water quality based on the Water Quality Index (WQI) and pollution categories.
What is the Water Quality Index (WQI)
The Water Quality Index (WQI) is a numerical value that reflects the quality of water in a specific location based on several parameters such as pH, the presence of harmful chemicals, and pollution levels. The lower the WQI value, the better the water quality.
How do I read the water quality report results?
Verified Locations: Marked with a special icon and reports that have been verified by the WaterGuard team or relevant authorities. WQI Categories: Each report includes a WQI category indicating the pollution level, such as "Lightly Polluted" or "Heavily Polluted".
Is my data safe with WaterGuard?
Yes, user data security is our top priority. WaterGuard uses the latest encryption technology to protect your personal information and ensure your data remains secure.
